Instructions

Object of the Game

The object of Connect 4 is to line up four of your color tokens (red or yelllow) in either a vertical, horizontal, or diagonal line.

How to Play

To play, simply click or tap the column you would like to place a token. It is red's turn first. When you click or tap, the color will automatically change to yellow. The first person to line up 4 in a row is the winner! Now, get out there and play!!!
